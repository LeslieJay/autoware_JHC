### ROS2中获取节点的 logger 和 clock

1. `logger_, *clock_` 
RCLCPP_INFO_THROTTLE(logger_, *clock_, 5000, "output is: ");

2. `get_logger(), *get_clock()`
RCLCPP_INFO_THROTTLE(get_logger(), *get_clock(), 5000, "output is: ");
